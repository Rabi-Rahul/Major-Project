About the Project

This project aims to build a real-time cyber threat intelligence platform focused on Indian cyberspace. It integrates automated vulnerability monitoring, cybercrime news aggregation, a SafeLink detector, and a public threat reporting portal to support critical sectors, security teams, and citizens.

Future enhancements include SIEM integration, ML-based risk scoring, honeypot data, threat APIs, digital forensics tools, and APIs for enterprise & government SOCs.
